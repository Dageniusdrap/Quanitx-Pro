# Privacy Policy for Quantix Pro

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Quantix Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is developed by **Talented Villagers**. We believe that your mathematical calculations and financial data are your own. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 2. No Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, share, or sell any of your personal data.**
*   We do not track your location.
*   We do not access your contacts.
*   We do not see your calculations.
*   We do not use usage analytics tools.

## 3. Local Data Storage
All operations are performed locally on your device. We use your device's native "Local Storage" only to remember your preferences:
*   Selected Theme (Dark/Light mode)
*   Last used Calculator Mode
*   Calculation History (stored temporarily on your device)

If you delete the app or clear your browser cache, this local data is removed. We never receive a copy of it.

## 4. No Third-Party SDKs
Quantix Pro is an ad-free experience. We do not include any advertising SDKs (Software Development Kits) or third-party tracking scripts.
